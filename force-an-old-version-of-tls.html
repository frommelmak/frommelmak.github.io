Force an old version of TLS

Mon 20 January 2020
By frommelmak
Category: English
Tags: apache ssl tls

If you -for whatever reason- need to force the old TLS-1.0, you can put the folllowing line en the Apache’s /etc/apache2/mods-enabled/ssl.conf file.

SSLProtocol All -TLSv1.1 -TLSv1.2

Note: TLS-1.0 is broken and is considered insecure. This version of the protocol will be rejected by the major browsers at some point during the current year. So, use this trick at your own risk. Tested on Ubuntu 12.04.
