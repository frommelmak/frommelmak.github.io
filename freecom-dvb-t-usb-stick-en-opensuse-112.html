Freecom DVB-T USB Stick en Opensuse 11.2

Tue 29 December 2009
By frommelmak
Category: Spanish
Tags: opensuse freecom dvb-t usb

Olvida Yast2. Lo más rápido es:

  • Descargar dvb-usb-wt220u-02.fw
  • Copiarlo a /lib/firmware
  • Pinchar Stick y mirar si aparece en /var/log/messages

Arrancamos KDE Media Player, configuramos el dispositivo y listos.

Proudly powered by Pelican, which takes great advantage of Python.
